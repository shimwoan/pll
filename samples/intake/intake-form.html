PERSONAL INJURY RETAINER AGREEMENT
Contingency Fee Agreement & Power of Attorney

This Retainer Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between [Client Name] ("Client") and PLL Law Group ("Firm"), in connection with a personal injury claim arising from an accident occurring on [Date of Loss] at or near [Location].

1. Scope of Representation

The Firm agrees to represent the Client in pursuing all claims for personal injury, property damage, and related damages arising from the above-referenced incident. Representation includes negotiation with insurance carriers, filing of claims, and, if necessary, litigation.

2. Contingency Fee

Client agrees to pay the Firm a contingency fee of 33⅓% of any gross recovery if the matter is resolved prior to the filing of a lawsuit, and 40% of any gross recovery if a lawsuit is filed. No attorney's fee is owed if there is no recovery.

3. Costs & Expenses

Client authorizes the Firm to advance costs on Client's behalf (e.g., medical records, filing fees, expert witnesses). All advanced costs shall be reimbursed to the Firm from any recovery, prior to distribution of net proceeds to Client.

4. Client Obligations

Client agrees to: (a) cooperate fully with the Firm; (b) keep the Firm informed of any change in address, employment, or medical treatment; (c) not discuss the claim with any insurance adjuster, opposing party, or their representatives without prior Firm approval.

5. Medical Treatment Authorization

Client authorizes the Firm to obtain medical records, billing statements, and treatment reports from all healthcare providers relevant to this claim. Client also authorizes healthcare providers to provide a lien on any settlement proceeds for unpaid medical bills.

6. Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement upon written notice. In the event of termination by the Client, the Firm shall be entitled to quantum meruit compensation for services rendered.

7. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California. Any dispute arising under this Agreement shall be subject to binding arbitration in Los Angeles County.

HIPAA Authorization for Release of Medical Records

I, [Client Name], hereby authorize any physician, hospital, clinic, therapist, or other healthcare provider to release to PLL Law Group and/or its designated agents complete medical records, itemized billing statements, diagnostic reports, and treatment notes related to injuries sustained on or after [Date of Loss].

This authorization includes, but is not limited to, records relating to: physical therapy, chiropractic care, orthopedic treatment, neurological evaluations, imaging (X-ray, MRI, CT), pharmaceutical records, and any mental health or behavioral health treatment directly related to the above incident.

This authorization is valid for one (1) year from the date signed, or until the conclusion of the legal matter, whichever is later. A copy or fax of this authorization shall be as valid as the original.

You have the right to revoke this authorization at any time by submitting written notice to PLL Law Group. Revocation will not affect disclosures already made in reliance on this authorization.

Medical Lien Authorization

Client authorizes all treating physicians, medical groups, hospitals, and healthcare providers to provide services on a lien basis. Client hereby assigns to such providers a security interest in any settlement, judgment, or award obtained in this matter, to the extent of any outstanding balances for treatment rendered.

This lien is subordinate to attorney's fees and costs. PLL Law Group is authorized to honor and pay any valid medical lien from settlement proceeds prior to disbursement of net proceeds to Client.

By signing below, Client acknowledges having read and understood this Agreement in its entirety, including the Contingency Fee, HIPAA Authorization, and Lien Authorization, and agrees to be bound by its terms.
